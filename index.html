ABOUT US

Toledo Ark Sports & Recreation center was founded in 2023 to give children ages 6-17 a place for mentorship , support and safety.

Toledo Ark has behavioral health programs centered around boxing , dance , and other recreational activities to ensure our children are learning and growing mentally and professionally.

Our goal is to provide a place where children will not be bullied , but instead respected , love and encouraged on a daily basis. Our Mission statement is to help children survive childhood.

OUR STORY

FOUNDER'S JOURNEY

From the streets of Toledo to building a sanctuary for youth - the inspiring story of Andre McCallum Jr.

ANDRE MCCALLUM JR.

ANDRE MCCALLUM JR WAS BORN & RAISED ON THE SOUTHSIDE OF TOLEDO OHIO. BY THE AGE OF 11 HE BECAME INVOLVED WITH GANGS, WAS EXPELLED FROM STEWART ELEMENTARY "PUBLIC SCHOOL" & WAS TOLD THAT HE WOULD BE JUST ANOTHER STATISTIC IN THE GHETTO...

BY THE GRACE OF GOD, ANDRE'S FIRST CHILD WAS BORN. NOW ONLY 19 YEARS OLD ANDRE WAS A FATHER. IMMEDIATELY, HIS FOCUS SHIFTED FROM THE STREETS & ANDRE WAS DETERMINED TO PROVIDE FOR HIS NEW BORN SON.

AFTER MONTHS OF WORKING MISCELLANEOUS DEAD END JOBS, ANDRE FINALLY RECEIVED HIS BREAK & WAS OFFERED A SALES POSITION IN THE INSURANCE INDUSTRY WHICH BEGAN HIS JOURNEY TO FINANCIAL FREEDOM.

13+ Years in Business
100s Mentored

FROM 2010 - 2023, ANDRE OWNED & OPERATED OFFICES THROUGHOUT THE UNITED STATES. HE BROKE SALES RECORDS & MENTORED HUNDREDS OF MEN & WOMEN IN THE INSURANCE & SALES INDUSTRY.

NOW, 36 YEARS OLD, ANDRE MCCALLUM IS A PUBLISHED AUTHOR, PHILANTHROPIST & FOUNDER, MENTOR & BOXING COACH AT TOLEDO ARK. HE HAS DEDICATED HIS LIFE TO HELP STOP VIOLENCE AND TO ENCOURAGE & MOTIVATE THE YOUTH TO TAKE CONTROL OF LIFE & TO DEFEAT THE ODDS!
